About AI Study Assistant

AI Study Assistant is a smart educational application designed for students to improve learning through Artificial Intelligence. It provides AI chat, voice assistant, PDF summarization, and AI quiz generation in one app. The goal is to simplify learning and improve productivity using modern AI tools.
